In Home Consultation

With our General In Home Consultation a technician will come to your home or business and help you out with anything you need. Examples of this can include configuring your router, helping set up a smart device or troubleshooting email issues.

- Have a technician show up to your home/business: $45

- First 30 min of consultation is free with an additional: $15 charged every quarter hour

Computer Repair/Services

Our technicians are trained on how to solve many different computer issues with Windows, Macs or Chromebooks. However, these problems are typically more involved and time consuming leading to us typically having to take these with us for 4-5 days.

A normal example of this timeline is a technician would consult with you on the day of the appointment to come to your location to ensure this is what is best for the situation. Once a good solution is identified, the technician will take only the necessary equipment with them to continue the repair. Please allow the technician 4-5 days to work towards completing the repair before scheduling a good time for them to return the computer and review the repairs.

Data transfer/ Data Copy: $50

Need to get files from one location to another? We can help! We can transfer your files to where they need regardless if its just to backup or to transfer on to a new computer.

Software Install: $60

Have a new software you've been wanting to try out but haven't had the time to install it? We would be happy to do it for you. We can try to set this software up to best suit your needs as well as ensure its functioning well.

General Tune Up: $80

If your computer not running up to your standards we can optimize the computer to its best ability. This would include searching for any viruses or malware installed on the computer, installing any needed updates to the OS or drivers and making sure all of the settings are set to the optimal settings.

Hard drive upgrade: $75 + cost of material

Hard drives are expendable just like the tires on your car, they often ware out over time. With this we can install a new hard drive in your computer allowing you to install your own OS of choice.

Data transfer/ Data Copy: $50

Need to get files from one location to another? We can help! We can transfer your files to where they need regardless if its just to backup or to transfer on to a new computer.

A Fresh OS install: $100 + potential cost of OS

When you need a computer to go back to a clean slate, reinstalling the operating system is your go to answer. This will remove all of the personal information you've entered to the computer over the years. Additionally, this is a sure way to remove any malware on the computer.

The Complete Package: $200 + cost of hardware

When you want to get a few more years out of a computer this is the route for you. This deal includes many of our services in one affordable package. This comes with installing a new Solid State hard drive in the computer. Installing a fresh copy up to date copy of the OS on the computer and transferring over the files from the old hard drive. Additionally, we install any software requested on the machine. This is our most comprehensive offer.

Hardware Repairs/Installs: $150* + cost of materials

We can service most hardware situations you can run across. From replacing laptop hinges to installing new mother boards we have your back.

* The price of this service is typically $150. However a different estimate can be given by the technician. Repair timeline is subject to part delivery times.
